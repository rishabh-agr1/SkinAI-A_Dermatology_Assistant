What is SkinAI and how does it help?

"SkinAI" is an innovative platform developed by students from B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE). It uses artificial intelligence to provide personalized skin care recommendations, analyzing user input to suggest tailored skincare routines, products, and tips.

SkinAI aims to help users make informed decisions about their skin health, improving both skin care and confidence.

Common Skin Diseases

Acne

Pimples, blackheads, and cysts caused by clogged hair follicles.

Eczema

Inflammatory skin condition causing itchy, red, dry, and cracked skin.

Psoriasis

Skin cells multiply rapidly, causing thick, scaly patches of skin.

Ringworm

Fungal infection leading to circular, red, itchy patches on skin.

Rosacea

Chronic condition causing redness, visible blood vessels.

Melanoma

A serious skin cancer originating in pigment-producing skin cells.
